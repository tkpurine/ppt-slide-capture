Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-17

Data Collection

PPT Slide Capture does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All data (captured slide images) is stored locally in your browser using chrome.storage.local and never leaves your device.

Permissions

  • activeTab – Required to capture the visible area of the current tab when you trigger a capture.
  • storage – Required to temporarily store captured images locally until you download them.
  • downloads – Required to save the ZIP file to your computer.

Third-Party Services

This extension does not use any third-party services, analytics, or advertising.

Contact

If you have any questions, please open an issue on GitHub.
